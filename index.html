The Aura Mechanism

Aura Finance is a decentralized Web3 banking protocol. Here is how our tokenomics and smart contracts power the ecosystem.

1. Tokenomics (AURO)

AURO is an ERC-20 standard utility token deployed on the Ethereum Sepolia Testnet. It acts as the backbone of the ecosystem.

  • Max Supply: 1,000,000,000 AURO
  • Distribution: 100% reserved for ecosystem liquidity and staking rewards.
  • No Bonding Curve: Ensures stable issuance without immediate market volatility.

2. Peer-to-Peer Finance

Traditional banking relies on centralized ledgers. Aura utilizes Web3 to allow permissionless, global value transfer.

  • Direct Routing: Funds move wallet-to-wallet via the smart contract.
  • Immutability: Every AURO transfer is publicly verifiable on the blockchain.

3. Smart Staking Yields

Users can lock their AURO tokens in the staking vault to secure the network and earn a targeted 12% APY.

The yield is generated mathematically by the smart contract, distributing newly minted AURO block-by-block proportionate to a user's share of the total staking pool.

4. Contract Infrastructure

Aura Finance interfaces directly with audited contracts deployed via Thirdweb, ensuring enterprise-grade security.
